Ingredients

  • Handmade pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Grated mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C). If using a pizza stone, preheat it in the oven at this time.
  2. Stretch pizza dough into a round or rectangular shape, depending on your preference. Place on a pizza peel dusted with cornmeal.
  3. Spread tomato sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a 1/2-inch border. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese on top.
  4. Transfer pizza to the preheated pizza stone, if using. Bake in the preheated oven until crust is golden brown and cheese has melted, 10 to 15 minutes.
  5. Remove pizza from the oven and top with fresh basil leaves. Slice and serve immediately.
